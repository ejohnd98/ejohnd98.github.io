Media Coverage

While working at Victoria Makerspace with Ocean Networks Canada, I got the chance to go to several tech conferences and events to demo "VR Tsunami," a virtual reality game which showcases ONC's ocean data and tsunami simulations, while raising awareness around tsunami preparedness. I, and my coworkers/managers, got to speak with a few different news sites and show off the project:

Global News - Virtual reality tsunami technology

Surrey Now-Leader - Virtual simulation engages users in the event of a tsunami hitting Port Alberni

CBC - 'Let's hope everyone made it out': virtual reality game immerses players in Port Alberni, B.C., tsunami
